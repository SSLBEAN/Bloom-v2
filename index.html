About Bloom

Bloom is a place for independent artists to plant a song and watch it grow. Upload a track, get real feedback on your vocals and lyrics, master it with a couple of taps, and share it with people who are looking for exactly what you make.

Unlike a black-box algorithm, Bloom shows its work: the loudness, tempo, and mood readings on every track come from analyzing the actual audio and lyrics you upload, not a guess.

This build is an early prototype. Some features you see (subscriptions, ads, full push notifications) are previews of where Bloom is headed rather than finished systems.
